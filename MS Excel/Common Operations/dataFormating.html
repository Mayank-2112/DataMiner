Data Formatting

Number Formatting

Step:-1Select the cell(s) or range you want to format.

Step:-2Right-click and choose "Format Cells" from the context menu or go to the "Home" tab, click on the "Number Format" drop-down list, and select "More Number Formats."

Step:-3In the "Format Cells" dialog box, choose the desired category (e.g., Number, Currency, Date, etc.) and select the appropriate options.

Step:-4Customize the formatting options such as decimal places, currency symbols, date formats, etc.

Step:-5Click "OK" to apply the formatting to the selected cells.
